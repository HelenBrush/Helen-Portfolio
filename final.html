Beebalm has delicate
purple blooms in the
early summer. After the
bloom dies, tap the
head and smell the
fragrant seeds before
you scatter them.
Indian grass grows beautiful
golden panicle in the late
summer. Look for it in dry
places, like the edge of
meadows.
Butterfly Milkweed is easy
to spot with bright orange
blooms in summer.Watch
Monarch butterflies and
caterpillars feed on the
nectar and leaves. In the
fall, gently open a seed-
pod and let the silk drift in
the wind!
